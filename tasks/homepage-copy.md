## Section 3 — Features (`id="features"`)

**Layout:** Alternating text-left/image-right on desktop. Stacked (headline + copy → screenshot) on mobile.

### Feature 1 — Bills
**Screenshot:** `lightmode-collapsed-bills.jpg`

**Headline:** Your recurring bills, all in one place.

**Body:**
> Loans, subscriptions, utilities, spending — Cyclar organizes every recurring bill into categories that make sense. Set a recurrence once, and Cyclar generates every upcoming due date automatically. Mark bills active or inactive as your life changes.

---

### Feature 2 — Manage
**Screenshot:** `lightmode-manage-screen.jpg`

**Headline:** See exactly what's due before your next paycheck.

**Body:**
> The Manage view shows your cashflow for whatever window matters to you — next paycheck, end of month, or a custom range. Bills are organized into Outstanding, Upcoming, and Paid so you always know where things stand. Add one-time expenses on the fly without creating permanent templates.

---

### Feature 3 — Analytics
**Screenshot:** `lightmode-analytics-2.jpg`

**Headline:** Understand where your money actually goes.

**Body:**
> Analytics shows your net cashflow, spending breakdown by category, and income vs. bills over time. Tap any chart to drill into the underlying transactions. Choose a range from the current month to the past year — or set a custom date window.

---

### Feature 4 — Debt Tracker
**Screenshot:** `lightmode-debt-overview-screen.jpg`

**Headline:** Watch your debt shrink over time.

**Body:**
> The Debt Tracker shows every loan's progress, projected payoff date, and payment history in one view. Log balance updates, track additional payments, and see compound interest estimates before you accept them. Progress bars show what you've paid off and how far you have left to go.

## Section 4 — Differentiators

**Layout:** Three cards in a row on desktop, stacked on mobile. Light gray card background.

### Card 1 — vs. automation-first apps
**Headline:** You know your finances. Not just your app.

**Body:**
> Automation-first tools track your money so you don't have to think about it. Cyclar takes the opposite approach — intentionally maintaining your own data builds real understanding. Over time, you trust yourself, not an algorithm.

---

### Card 2 — vs. spreadsheets
**Headline:** Feels like an app. Works like one.

**Body:**
> Spreadsheets break when life changes. Cyclar is built for mobile from the ground up — large tap targets, thumb-friendly navigation, instant feedback. The layout adapts as your bills change, no maintenance required.

---

### Card 3 — No bank linking
**Headline:** No linking required.

**Body:**
> Cyclar doesn't connect to your bank accounts. You enter what you want, when you want. No Plaid, no third-party sync, no permissions to revoke. You maintain the picture; you own the clarity.
