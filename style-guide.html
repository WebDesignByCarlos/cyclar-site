Shadows

Three levels of elevation. Use sparingly — every element casting a shadow competes for attention. Prefer --shadow-sm for cards, --shadow-md for dropdowns, --shadow-lg for modals and featured elements.

Used for most cards and surface elements.

Alerts & Notices

Used for feedback after an action — not as ambient decoration. Alerts appear in response to something the user just did, and disappear when the state resolves. Titles are direct; body text explains what happened and what to do next.

✓
Payment recorded
Your $120 rent payment has been marked as paid.
!
Due in 2 days
Your $65 internet bill is due on June 20. Mark it paid when you've sent the payment.
×
Payment overdue
Your $240 rent payment was due June 1 and hasn't been marked paid. Check your outstanding bills.
